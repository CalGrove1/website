About Me

Hi! My name is Callahan Grove (I go by Cal) and I am a audio production major at Minnesota State University Moorhead. I am from the twin cities and I love music! I have been producinng music since I was 12 making beats on an old macbook. I currently work here and there as a stagehand for Mobile Pro, and I have worked the sound for events for SPPS in Saint Paul.

I want you!

Are you an up and coming band in the Fargo-Moorhead area that would like to be recorded? I can be there in a jiffy and have the rough mixes back in a week! I've got all the equipment you need to be recorded! Just bring your intruments and your music and I will handle the rest. I don't charge for now, so all you have to do is play! Fill out my contact form linked below to get in touch. Hope to talk to you soon!
